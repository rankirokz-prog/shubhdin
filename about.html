ShubhDin — शुभ दिन
Your Daily Hindu Devotional Companion

ShubhDin is a daily spiritual companion app designed for Hindi-speaking Hindu devotees. Our mission is simple — help you start every day with divine awareness, mantra practice, and panchang guidance.

What ShubhDin Offers

🔊
Daily Panchang Audio
Personalized panchang narration with your name, tithi, nakshatra, shubh muhurta, and rahu kaal — read aloud in warm Hindi voice every morning.
🪔
Daily Jap Practice
Deity-specific mantra for each day of the week — Mahadev on Monday, Shri Hanuman on Tuesday, and more. Synced word-by-word highlight with audio.
📿
Sadhana Tracker
21-day to 40-day sadhana journeys for marriage, wealth, health, education, and home harmony. Track your daily progress and streak.
📅
Full Panchang
Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana, Sunrise, Sunset, Rahu Kaal, and Abhijit Muhurta — updated daily from live astrology data for your city.

Our Vision

ShubhDin was built with the belief that spirituality should be simple, personal, and accessible. In a world of distractions, we want to give every devotee a calm, focused moment of divine connection every morning.

"No astrologer can solve your problems — only Shiva can."
