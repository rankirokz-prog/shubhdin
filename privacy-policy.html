Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

ShubhDin ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Name — to personalize your daily panchang greeting
  • Date of Birth — to calculate your Rashi (zodiac sign)
  • City — to provide accurate local panchang timings
  • Language preference — Hindi or English

All this information is stored only on your device (localStorage). We do not store your personal data on our servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect email addresses
  • We do not collect phone numbers
  • We do not collect location data or GPS
  • We do not track your browsing activity
  • We do not use advertising cookies or trackers
  • We do not share your data with third parties

3. Audio Generation

Your name is sent to ElevenLabs (a text-to-speech service) solely to generate a personalized audio greeting. Your name is not stored or used by ElevenLabs for any other purpose beyond this single audio generation request.

4. Panchang Data

We use your city to fetch daily panchang data from a third-party astrology API. Only the city name is sent — no personally identifiable information is transmitted.

5. Data Storage

All your personal preferences (name, city, DOB, language) are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. You can delete this data at any time by clearing your browser/app data.

6. Children's Privacy

ShubhDin is a devotional app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
